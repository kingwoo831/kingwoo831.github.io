Our Survey

Survey An online survey is distributed to local Hong Kong born residents. Our target survey respondents are age 20 to 25, local university undergraduate and postgraduate students. In the survey, we have divided our questions into four sections: Personal information, understanding on sex education, understanding on sex education according to the catergories created by the government, relationship status, situation during sex and after-sex measures and situation after baby is born. We have included various question types, such as closed-ended questions, checkbox questions, preference ratings and open-ended questions, in order to construct a more thorough sampling group. We have collected 42 responses in total. The result please refer to our report.
